=============
Shaheen III
=============

Shaheen III is an HPE Cray EX supercomputer consisting of two partitions: a CPU partition and a GPU-accelerated partition. The CPU partition comprises 4,608 AMD Genoa compute nodes, each offering 192 cores and 384 GB of memory, and delivers 35.6 PFlop/s of sustained HPL performance. The GPU partition includes 2,800 NVIDIA GH200 Grace Hopper Superchips and achieved 122.8 PFlop/s of HPL performance. This makes the Shaheen III GPU system the fastest supercomputer in the Middle East at the time of its installation and places it among the worldâ€™s top systems, currently ranked 18th on the Top500 list (November 2025).
